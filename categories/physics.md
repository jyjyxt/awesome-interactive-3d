# Physics Interactive Concepts

Interactive physics visualizers for connecting motion equations to changing
geometric and graph-based representations.

## Projectile Motion

- URL: <https://figviz.com/concepts/projectile-motion>
- Interactive: <https://figviz.com/concepts/projectile-motion>
- Level: High school, College
- Teaches: how launch angle and speed determine trajectory, range, maximum
  height, and time of flight.
- Interaction: drag launch angle and initial speed to update the parabolic path
  and velocity components.
- Exports: PNG, GIF, MP4.

## Simple Harmonic Motion

- URL: <https://figviz.com/concepts/simple-harmonic-motion>
- Interactive: <https://figviz.com/concepts/simple-harmonic-motion>
- Level: High school, College
- Teaches: how amplitude, period, frequency, and angular frequency shape
  sine-wave displacement.
- Interaction: drag amplitude and period to reshape the oscillation.
- Exports: PNG, GIF, MP4.
